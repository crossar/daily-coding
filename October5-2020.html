Astral World

Plot:

Based on zodiac signs living in a strange world with a lot of different creatures. Vy, the princess, doesn’t have an idea about her hidden power that can be a threat or protection to people around her. What will happen?

Main Characters:

  1. Azariah Libzewick (Azee)
  2. Viivi Fryzell (Vy)
  3. Luzveth Belarus (Luz)
  4. Gwinnett Vos (Gwen)
  5. Glanvyll Vos (Glen)
